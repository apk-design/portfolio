Ability Selection and Subsequent Restriction

This feature redesigned the Ability Selection experience and introduced a new Ability Select Restriction system. I collaborated with a contracted pro gamer, the UI team, and gameplay stakeholders to create a clearer, more intuitive, visually expressive workflow for selecting abilities while communicating team-wide restrictions transparently.

UI/UX Web Design On Site Job Applications

The Brief

Players were experiencing confusion around ability availability during team formation. The previous system did not clearly communicate why certain abilities were unavailable, leading to frustration. The goal was to make restrictions intuitive, visible, and fair while showcasing the game‚Äôs strong visual identity.

Early UX Exploration

Early UX mapping included Team Composition states, ability grouping by faction, selection-state logic, player tags, and multiple edge cases. These wireframes focused on clarity, readability, and competitive usability while supporting controller and mouse input.

Initial Ability Selection UI Design

After establishing the UX foundation, I explored UI layouts that emphasized:

  • Large, expressive character art
  • Strong visual identity for abilities
  • Clear faction navigation
  • Improved readability at various screen sizes
  • Layouts flexible enough for future ability expansions

Additions & Stakeholder Feedback

Working closely with the pro gamer, UI artists, and gameplay designers, we refined the system to include better tooltips, improved iconography, faction lock logic, error-prevention states, and clearer real-time updates when players selected or deselected abilities.

Final UX (Ability Selection + Restriction Combined)

The final UX combined competitive clarity and strong visuals. It featured real-time lockouts, player tagging on selected abilities, faction grouping, and intuitive messaging that prevented errors before they happened.

Final UI in Game

After UX approval, the UI artist translated the system into a polished, high-fidelity design. The final UI introduced dynamic backgrounds, detailed character renders, and strong color hierarchy‚Äîall while preserving UX clarity.

Impact

  • Reduced confusion around ability availability
  • Improved team coordination visibility
  • Faster selections in competitive modes
  • Clearer onboarding for new players
  • Future-proofed layout for new abilities and skins
  • Strengthened visual and systemic consistency across the game
