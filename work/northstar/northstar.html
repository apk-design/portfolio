North Star Wellness Resources

A 24-hour design exercise for an interview brief: create a responsible, empathetic experience for people seeking mental health or substance-use resources. The challenge was to prioritize what truly matters when time and information are limited.

Healthcare UX Branding

The site uses a predominantly dark interface on purpose. For a lot of people, especially those dealing with stigma or fear around needing help, visibility itself can feel uncomfortable.
